00 · Prologue

Beneath the world you know,
another is always turning.

Every night, your psyche stages a drama for an audience of one — and every morning it dissolves. Olorin is the door to that place, and a steady hand once you're through it.

01 · The Dream

It begins with a fragment.

Half-remembered. Already fading.

Speak it the instant you wake. Olorin transcribes, remembers, and surfaces the figures and symbols that recur across months — the threads you'd otherwise lose by breakfast.

02 · The Shadow

You meet a figure with something dark upon its back.

It has been following you for years.

Through guided Active Imagination, meet the parts of you that act from the dark — and give them form, so they stop running the show unseen.

03 · The Dialogue

You speak. And to your surprise, it answers.

Read the dream through three lenses — Freudian, Jungian, Dream Yoga.

Offered as perspectives to sit with, never as verdicts. The conversation is where the shadow becomes an ally.

04 · The Stillness

Between the words, you learn to simply watch.

Steady the watching mind. A breath you can see — in the Himalayan tradition of the Swami Rama lineage. No streaks to chase. Just a light to return to.

05 · The Commons

You look up — and find you were never alone.

"If it were my dream…"

Share a dream and hear how it lands in others — the old dream-circle ethic, rebuilt for a quieter kind of social. A window to the collective unconscious.

06 · The Return

You climb back into daylight,
no longer nameless.

The patterns rise — and so do you.

This is individuation: adversity turned to teacher, the dark made legible, the self made whole. All of it sealed on your device — encrypted before it leaves your phone. No one holds a key but you.

The door is open

Begin the descent.
